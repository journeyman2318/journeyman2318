⭐ GRAVITON WAVE — PHENOMENON ENTRY

Registry Classification: CAT‑Ω / Extinction‑Level Event

Status: Active (Ongoing Propagation)

Primary Source: Xryaxis Stellar Hyper‑Collapse

Monitoring Authority: Triad Scientific Council

Hazard Rating: Omega‑Class

📘 1. Overview

The Graviton Wave is the single most destructive astrophysical event in recorded history — a galaxy‑wide spatial destabilization triggered by the collapse of the Xryaxis star. It is responsible for the loss, isolation, or mutation of dozens of systems and the collapse of pre‑Wave civilization.

Unlike a traditional gravitational wave, this phenomenon carried coherent graviton structures capable of:

  • destabilizing atomic bonds
  • collapsing wildspace boundaries
  • shredding magical fields
  • disrupting FTL corridors
  • causing system‑scale spatial folding

🧪 2. Origin: The Xryaxis Collapse

The event began when the Xryaxis star underwent a catastrophic hyper‑collapse, generating:

  • a graviton surge orders of magnitude beyond theoretical limits
  • a wildspace implosion that caused neighboring zones to collide
  • a coherent graviton pulse that continues to ripple throughout the galaxy

🌀 3. Propagation Behavior

The Wave did not behave like a normal ripple. It:

  • accelerated when passing through dense magical regions
  • fragmented into pulses when interacting with wildspace boundaries
  • reflected off certain stellar phenomena
  • left behind “graviton scars” — permanent distortions in spacetime

This explains why some systems were obliterated instantly, while others suffered slow destabilization.

☠️ 4. Systems Destroyed or Lost to the Wave

Curpum & Plumbie — The Heavy Flux

Twin systems believed to have been struck early. The region is now surrounded by a dense cloud of hazardous particulate matter.

Ferron — Industrial Hub Collapse

Once a major manufacturing center, Ferron’s planetary crusts destabilized and imploded. The system is now an uninhabitable debris field of Wimu Corporation remnants.

The A‑Z Industries Main Facility

It is unknown whether the Wave caused the A‑Z blackout and the formation of the surrounding halo void.

The Hexa‑Spire Cluster

Six tightly bound stars whose shared gravity amplified the Wave. All three collapsed into a tangled mass of exotic matter.

The Myridian Reach

A frontier agricultural region where the Wave destabilized water at the molecular level, turning oceans into volatile gas clouds.

The Kessari Fold

A system famous for its natural wormholes. The Wave caused all wormholes to collapse simultaneously, tearing the system apart and releasing a lethal cadmium‑2 radiation surge.

🟡 5. Systems Damaged but Surviving

Hygenium — Food System

The system survived, but surrounding space was heavily altered. Spelljamming is now extremely difficult and dangerous.

Argentum — Frontier System

Spatial distortion thinned the atmosphere of its only inhabited world. Travel in the region remains hazardous due to unstable space.

🕳️ 6. FLASH Gate Network

The Wave created multiple regions of space that are dangerous to SpellJam through. While travel remains possible, even slight interaction with Wave remnants can cause catastrophic injury or death.

In response, the Triad worlds pooled resources to map the remaining stable corridors and installed the FLASH (Faster than Light Area Super Highway) gates to connect them into a unified travel network.

🧩 7. Known Scientific Properties

The Wave exhibits:

  • coherent graviton structures (behave like particles and waves simultaneously)
  • temporal echoes (signals from the past or future)
  • spatial folding (regions overlapping themselves)
  • magic‑field inversion (spelljammers destabilize instantly)
  • matter phase‑shift (objects flicker between states)
