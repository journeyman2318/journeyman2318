Help! I Don't Exist

If you are alive, alive-adjacent, or undead, you must be registered with the Registry of Sentient Beings.

You may visit any RSB Hub Site or travel to Regula and the RSB Spire to register your existence.

If you were alive, then decided not to be, and have since changed your mind or were brought back:
You must complete Form 88-I (Intent to Request Reinstatement of Living Status).

If you were not alive and now are:
You must complete Form 88-A (Initial Adknowledgement of Sentience)
-or- Form 88-P (Initial Post-Mortem Reanimation Acknowledgment).

If you were not alive and still are not alive, but possess sentience:
You must submit Form 88-M (Manifested Sentience Declaration).

Questions?
Please enter your RSB-ID on the main page for additional information, or visit an RSB Clerk for immediate assistance.
