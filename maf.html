⭐ MORRIS–ALEKSTON FOUNDATION — ORGANIZATIONAL ENTRY

Registry Classification: MA‑FND / Tier‑1 Trans‑System Philanthropic‑Industrial Entity

Status: Active

Headquarters: Aurum System, Triad Region

Oversight: Galactic Oversight (Central Organized Government), Corporate‑Philanthropic Division

Hazard Rating: Administrative (Low), Research (Variable), Security (Moderate)

🏛️ 1. Overview

The Morris–Alekston Foundation (MAF) is one of the oldest continuously operating philanthropic‑industrial entities in the Triad. Its origins predate most modern governments, though the Foundation’s official records are curiously vague about the exact year of incorporation.

Publicly, the Foundation presents itself as a benevolent guardian of civilization — “helping you, whether you know it or like it.”

Privately, the Foundation has shifted from charity to strategic intervention, using its vast resources to prepare for the encroaching Wave. Its headquarters on Aurum houses advanced research labs, containment vaults, and a security apparatus more suited to a sovereign state than a charity.

The Foundation’s public messaging emphasizes protection, stability, and continuity. Its internal documents emphasize survival.

👥 2. Founders

Jalica Morris — Chair of External Relations

Jalica Morris is celebrated across the Triad as a visionary humanitarian whose commitment to cultural preservation and intersystem cooperation has shaped generations. Her speeches — rare, elegant, and delivered with calm certainty — emphasize unity, compassion, and the belief that every civilization deserves a future worth inheriting.

Her presence carries a timeless quality. Archival images from decades past show the same poised figure, the same steady gaze, the same quiet confidence. Supporters call her the heart of the Foundation — a guiding light whose empathy extends far beyond any single world or era.

Official Foundation Statement:
“Jalica reminds us that care is not a resource to be rationed. It is a responsibility to be upheld.”

Daydus Alekston — Director of Internal Operations

Daydus Alekston is known as the Foundation’s architect — the mind behind its vast logistical networks, research initiatives, and long‑term planning strategies. Where Jalica inspired, Daydus ensured. His meticulous attention to detail and uncanny foresight allowed the Foundation to anticipate crises long before they escalated.

Public appearances became rare, but when he spoke, his words carried the weight of someone who has watched civilizations rise, fall, and rise again. Colleagues described him as steady, patient, and unwavering in his belief that preparation is the purest form of compassion.

Official Foundation Statement:
“Daydus builds the structures that protect us — even from dangers we have yet to understand.”

A Love That Became a Mission

The story of the Morris–Alekston Foundation begins not with a boardroom, but with a partnership — a bond forged in shared purpose. Jalica and Daydus met during a relief effort on a frontier world, where their combined efforts stabilized a failing colony. Those who were there still speak of the moment the two first stood side by side: a sense that something larger had just begun.

Their relationship became the Foundation’s founding principle: love expressed as protection, scaled to the size of a galaxy.

Over time, their partnership grew into a philosophy:

  • Care is action.
  • Protection is love made practical.
  • A safer galaxy is a shared inheritance.

Their devotion to one another became devotion to everyone. Today, the Foundation proudly cites their story as proof that compassion — when paired with resolve — can reshape the stars themselves.

🜚 3. Mission Statement

“We safeguard the future you depend on.”

🛡️ 4. Public Posture

The Foundation’s outward stance is one of firm, paternalistic benevolence. They do not ask for permission. They do not wait for consensus. They act.

Their messaging frames this as a moral duty:

  • “We intervene where others hesitate.”
  • “We protect those who cannot protect themselves.”
  • “We make the difficult choices so you don’t have to.”

This posture has earned them admiration, resentment, and a quiet, simmering fear.

🏰 6. Headquarters: Aurum

A monumental citadel built atop a storm‑lashed plateau. The storm has hovered there for as long as anyone can remember.

Notable Features

  • Air‑curtain entry system that dries visitors instantly
  • Massive ceremonial doors that open at a deliberate, intimidating pace
  • Rotunda with holographic banners and a single, perpetually stressed halfling receptionist
  • Path‑lighting that activates only when visitors are expected

💠 7. Known Assets & Holdings

  • A fleet of research vessels
  • Custodial rights to many rare treasures (Museum of Tempes)
