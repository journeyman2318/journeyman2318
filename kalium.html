🧭 1. System Overview

Kalium is the computational heart of the Triad Scientific Society.

Where:

  • Oganesson pursues theory
  • Aurum refines materials
  • Kalium builds the systems that make everything work

For centuries, Kalium has specialized in:

  • advanced computer engineering
  • systems architecture
  • Multitronic logic design
  • early AI frameworks
  • post‑photonic integration
  • shuttle avionics and control systems

The planet is a dense mesh of:

  • research parks
  • fabrication towers
  • logic‑foundry megastructures

Kalium’s culture is practical, efficient, and deeply rooted in problem‑solving.

🧠 2. Historical Significance — The Multitronic Era

Before photonic processors became standard, Kalium was the birthplace of Multitronic AI design.

The earliest:

  • heuristic cores
  • synergistic logic meshes
  • adaptive learning matrices
  • pre‑photonic starship AIs

…all originated here.

This includes the design lineage that eventually produced the MISTI line with the backing of A‑Z Industries.

Kalium’s archives contain:

  • original Multitronic schematics
  • deprecated firmware
  • compatibility layers
  • early AI ethics frameworks
  • maintenance protocols for legacy systems

Most of this knowledge is obscure and rarely accessed — but still preserved.
