🏢 1. Overview

A‑Z Industries was once the most powerful, most innovative, and most secretive megacorporation in the Tempes Galaxy. Its influence spanned:

  • starship design
  • industrial megastructures
  • experimental energy systems
  • early Wave‑era research
  • Multitronic AI integration
  • high‑risk planetary testing

A‑Z was not just a company — it was an era.

And then, without warning, it shuttered. No explanation. No public statement. No transition of power. Just silence.

The galaxy has been living in the shadow of that silence ever since.

🟦 2. Corporate Identity

A‑Z Industries was known for:

  • sleek industrial design
  • tactile‑only control interfaces
  • functional realism over luxury
  • brutal efficiency
  • absolute secrecy
  • a culture of precision and restraint

Unofficial motto: “If it works, it ships.”

Their ships, stations, and prototypes were built to last centuries — and many still do. A‑Z engineering is instantly recognizable: clean lines, exposed structure, and a quiet confidence that the machine will outlive its operator.

🟧 3. Major Facilities & Worlds

A‑Z’s influence is scattered across the entire map. The corporation left fingerprints everywhere — some intentional, some accidental.

A‑Z Headquarters — Halo Void

Now a starless void region. The former headquarters sit inside a 1‑parsec radius Null Halo, a perfect sphere devoid of starlight. No one knows how the Halo was created — or if A‑Z deployed it.

Cuprum — Experimental Test World

Used for high‑risk energy experiments, Wave‑containment prototypes, and megastructure trials. Now fully encased in Heavy Flux. All records of Cuprum’s final days are corrupted or missing.

Plumbia — Minimal Testing, Maximum Mystery

A‑Z performed only light ecological studies here. Yet the Heavy Flux originated on Plumbia, not Cuprum. This contradiction remains one of the greatest scientific mysteries in the galaxy.

Hygenium

Hygenium benefited from A‑Z Industries, who graciously offered advanced agricultural tech.

Aurum — Material Science Partnerships

A‑Z collaborated with Aurum’s metallurgists on early starship alloys, including the prototypes that led to the Cyprus‑Class.

Oganesson — Academic Ties

A‑Z recruited heavily from Oganesson’s scientific academies. Entire graduating classes vanished into A‑Z’s research divisions.

Kalium — Manufacturing & Development

A‑Z contracted heavily from Kalium for their strict manufacturing tolerances and quality. They collaborated on Multitronics and early photonic systems.

🟥 4. The Collapse

A‑Z Industries did not fall — it vanished.

Known Facts

  • All facilities shut down within days
  • All research ceased
  • All personnel disappeared or were reassigned
  • All public records were sealed
  • All corporate assets were abandoned

The only event tied to the collapse: Afaxian Gatlunamort’s disappearance.

When he vanished, the empire crumbled. Entire systems fell into darkness. Wimu rose to fill the void. The galaxy has never recovered.

Some believe Afaxian triggered a failsafe. Some believe he ascended. Some believe he died. No theory fits all the evidence.

🟪 5. Legacy Technologies

A‑Z’s innovations still shape the galaxy:

Cyprus‑Class Ships

Durable, elegant, deceptively simple. Built for longevity, not comfort.

Early Wave‑Containment Concepts

Primitive, dangerous, and now lost. Some believe these experiments contributed to the Flux.

Multitronic‑Integrated Systems

A‑Z’s Multitronic architecture influenced Kalium’s entire engineering legacy.

Megastructure Trials on Cuprum

Now sealed forever inside the Heavy Flux. Whatever they were building may still be there.

Halo Void Infrastructure

The Halo Void is virtually impenetrable. Any attempt to enter results in ships exiting the void along their initial vector with no input from the ship or crew.

🟫 6. Cultural Impact

A‑Z Industries is remembered as:

  • a golden age of engineering
  • a symbol of restraint and humility
  • a mystery that still haunts the galaxy
  • a cautionary tale about power and secrecy

Some worlds revere A‑Z. Some fear it. Some pretend it never existed. But everyone feels the absence.

A‑Z is the ghost in the machine — the missing chapter the galaxy refuses to close.
