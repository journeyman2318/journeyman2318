🜂 1. System Overview

The Xyraxis Black Hole marks the location of the former Xyraxis Star, once the heart of the Astral Elf civilization. The system collapsed during a period of intense stellar instability, resulting in the formation of a singularity and the loss of all planetary bodies.

Modern consensus attributes the collapse to a failure in the empire’s stellar‑stabilization network during a period of external interference. The region is now a restricted navigation zone due to extreme gravitational distortion.

💥 2. The Collapse Event

Triad Scientific Society Classification: Catastrophic Stellar Failure

Status: Irrecoverable

📉 Official Account

Astral Elf scientists developed crystalline vine‑technology to reinforce their declining star. During the final stabilization phase, an unidentified rogue crew infiltrated the system, disrupting the delicate restoration process.

Despite the Emperor’s attempt to negotiate a peaceful resolution, the interference resulted in the collapse of the Xyraxis Star and the destruction of the system.

🌊 3. The Wave Phenomenon

Classification: Spatial Distortion Front

Origin: Xyraxis Collapse Event

📡 Observed Effects

  • atomic destabilization
  • ecosystem collapse
  • structural degradation of matter
  • unpredictable propagation through wildspace

The Wave is considered a natural byproduct of the collapse and remains one of the most destructive phenomena in the galaxy.

🧭 4. Astral Elf Cultural Legacy

The Astral Elf diaspora preserves the memory of Xyraxis as a symbol of resilience and scientific ambition. Cultural traditions emphasize:

  • reverence for lost knowledge
  • the pursuit of harmony between magic and science
  • the tragedy of the empire’s final days

Many diaspora communities consider the collapse a cautionary tale about interference and destabilization.

🏛️ 5. Museum Narrative

The Museum of Tempes on Regula hosts the exhibit “The Xaraxie Empire — The Last Days Before the Wave”, sponsored by the Reemus Group. The exhibit presents a curated account of the empire’s final era.

🎭 Key Themes

  • the star’s decline as a natural process
  • crystalline vines as sustainable stabilization technology
  • the rogue crew as unidentified saboteurs
  • the Emperor’s final appeal for peace

The exhibit concludes with a reminder of the dangers of misinformation and unregulated intervention.

🚫 6. Restricted Navigation Zone

Xyraxis is surrounded by a high‑intensity distortion field. All travel within the region is prohibited except for authorized scientific missions.

⚠️ Hazards

  • gravitational shear
  • temporal drift pockets
  • sensor disruption
  • corridor instability

🧪 7. Scientific Notes

Research platforms positioned at safe distances study the singularity’s behavior. Findings include:

  • irregular accretion patterns
  • sub‑space resonance anomalies
  • Wave‑echo signatures

No evidence supports claims that the collapse was artificially induced.

🌐 8. Travel & Corridor Advisory

No FLASH gates service the region. All major corridors bend outward to avoid distortion interference.

Corridor Notes

  • Zenith Corridor rerouted after early Wave Era
  • Triad Arcway maintains maximum safe distance

🔭 9. Historical Summary

  • Pre‑Collapse: Xyraxis was the Astral Elf capital system.
  • Stabilization Era: Crystalline vine‑technology deployed.
  • Interference Event: Rogue crew infiltrates system.
  • Collapse: Star implodes; Wave originates.
  • Modern Era: System designated as a restricted remnant.
