🌍 1. System Overview

Plumbia was once a lush, uninhabited forest world — a place of towering canopies, deep river valleys, and untouched wilderness. It was considered one of the most beautiful unclaimed worlds in the region.

Then the Heavy Flux appeared.

Plumbia shows no evidence of Wave interaction:

  • no Wavefront ever passed through
  • no Wave scars exist in local space
  • no historical records mention Wave anomalies

And yet, the Heavy Flux began here.

🌀 2. Origin of the Heavy Flux

Plumbia is widely believed to be the point of origin for the Heavy Flux phenomenon that now engulfs the entire region — including its sister world, Cuprum.

Key Observations

  • Flux emergence began on Plumbia’s surface
  • It expanded outward into orbit
  • It spread across the system
  • It eventually encased Cuprum
  • It continues to pulse with low‑frequency spatial distortions

The Flux here is denser, older, and more reactive than anywhere else in the galaxy.

COG Classification: “Heavy Flux Epicenter — DO NOT APPROACH.”

🌲 3. Pre‑Flux Plumbia

Before the anomaly, Plumbia was:

  • green
  • vibrant
  • ecologically rich
  • geologically stable
  • untouched by industry
  • considered for future colonization

A‑Z Industries performed only minimal testing here — nothing on the scale of Cuprum.

There is no known reason for the Flux to have chosen Plumbia.

⚠️ 4. Current State

Plumbia is now:

  • shrouded in Heavy Flux
  • impossible to scan
  • impossible to land on
  • impossible to observe directly
  • surrounded by spatial distortions
  • emitting irregular temporal echoes

The Flux behaves differently here:

  • it “breathes”
  • it pulses
  • it reacts to proximity
  • it distorts time and memory
  • it occasionally reveals glimpses of the surface

These glimpses are inconsistent, unreliable, and often contradictory.

🧪 5. Scientific Mystery

Plumbia is the single greatest anomaly in the COG’s scientific records.

Unresolved Questions

  • Why did the Flux originate here?
  • Why did it spread to Cuprum?
  • Why is it stable but reactive?
  • Why is it not Wave‑related?
  • What lies beneath the Flux?
  • What happened to the forest world?

No scientific expedition has ever returned with usable data.

The Triad eventually abandoned all research attempts.

🛑 6. COG Advisory

DO NOT APPROACH

The Triad’s warnings include:

  • extreme temporal distortion
  • gravitational shear
  • sensor blindness
  • memory discontinuity
  • multiple lost vessels
  • no rescue authorization

Approaching Plumbia is considered a fatal error.
